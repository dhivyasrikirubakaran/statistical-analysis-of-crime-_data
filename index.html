Methodology

Our statistical analysis is based on crime data collected from law enforcement agencies across multiple jurisdictions. The data undergoes a rigorous cleaning and standardization process before analysis.

Data Sources: Our primary data sources include official crime reports from local police departments, FBI Uniform Crime Reporting (UCR) program data, and supplemental data from court records.

Analysis Methods: We employ various statistical techniques including descriptive statistics, time series analysis, correlation analysis, and spatial analysis to identify patterns and trends in crime data.

Limitations: Crime data may be subject to reporting biases, changes in reporting practices over time, and variations in crime classification across jurisdictions.
